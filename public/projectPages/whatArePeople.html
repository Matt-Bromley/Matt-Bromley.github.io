What are People?

Project Description

This was a digital media piece assigned as a personal project to explore our skills and beliefs as creators. Each student was tasked with creating a piece in their form of digital Media. Being a Game Developer I created this small story based, point and click game to explore the topics the project was to covered.

The topic assigned to us was the topic of "imago dei" (image of God). As creators we were to explore what that looked like to us. One last requierment was that our project resemble the style of a tryptic

Project Details

  • Roles: Gameplay & Level Designer, Programmer
  • Collaborators: None
  • IDE: Processing 3
  • Design: Hobbyte

Gameplay

What are People? is a isometric, point and click puzzle game. There are three puzzles in total to reflect the tryptic art style, and each puzzle represents a different aspect of my interpretation of imago Dei. Players may control the character by clicking around on screen and various objects.

The goal of the game is to escape this mysterious holding place you have woken up in. After some exploring the player can find other people in this place that can help explain what is happening and hint to the player that there is a way to escape. If the player manages to collect all the fragments of a center piece to the main room the way out will be revieled. In order to do this they will need to complete the challenges of the three puzzles around the map, each one relating to imago Dei.
