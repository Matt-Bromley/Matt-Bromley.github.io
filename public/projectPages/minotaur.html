Minotaur

Project Description

Minotaur Is a top down maze runner game that I, with a group of fellow students designed for my Intro to Game Development class in 2018. Players may choose one of four characters to test their might in the maze. Each character has there own unique story and motivation for risking their lives in the maze, and each of them come with a unique ability to aid them in their encounters with the mighty Minotaur.

Project Details

  • Roles: Gameplay Designer, Programmer
  • Collaborators: Michael Williams (Character Art and Story), Bryce Donde (Art and Design), Josh Coldsmith (Level Design, Programmer)
  • IDE: Processing 3

Gameplay

The objective of Minotaur is to find all the hidden trasures thoughout the maze and escape with your new found wealth. However, watch out for traps, and most importantly, watch out for the Minotaur. Traps and the Minotaur will deal damage to the player. If the players heart meater is reduced to zero then the game is over and the player has lost.

Each character has one special ability that will aid them in any confrontation they may have to face.
